About Me

Hello! My name is Yusein Ali, and I'm 33 years old.

My Life Journey

I was born in Bulgaria, in the beautiful city of Burgas. In 2001, I moved with my parents to Spain, where I completed my secondary education and lived with them until 2010. Following that, I ventured to Turkey for a couple of months to pursue my passion for football.

After my time in Turkey, I returned to Bulgaria, where I played football for three years. In 2013, I took a significant step in my life by moving to Luxembourg. I lived there and continued to play football until 2023, when my wife and I decided to relocate to Sweden.

A Bit About Me

I have a border collie named Molly, who brings joy and energy into our lives. I’m multilingual, speaking Bulgarian, Spanish, French, English, Turkish, Italian, Portuguese, and Luxembourgish, and I am currently learning Swedish.

My Passion for Technology

Since childhood, I’ve always been fascinated by computers, programming, and everything surrounding technology. In 2023, I decided to embark on a new journey by enrolling in an online school, Code Institute, to enhance my programming skills.

About This Forum

This discussion forum is my fourth project out of a total of five that I have pursued through Code Institute. The idea for the Discussion Room Forum was inspired by a popular Spanish forum called Forocoches.com, which is one of the largest forum sites in Spain. It became a go-to place for me during tough days, as it provided a space where I could read jokes and funny posts that always brought me laughter and positivity.

Thank you for visiting my forum. I hope you find a welcoming space here to share and explore ideas with others!
